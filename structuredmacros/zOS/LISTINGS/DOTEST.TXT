                                      86 *                      THE "DO" STATEMENT                             * 00530000
                                      87 *                                                                     * 00540000
                                      88 *      The "DO" MACRO PROVIDES A LOOPING STRUCTURE FOR PERFORMING     * 00550000
                                      89 *      MULTIPLE PASSES THROUGH THE DESIRED CODE. THE "DO" HAS         * 00560000
                                      90 *      ADDITIONAL PARAMETERS OVER THE "IF", BUT USES THE SAME         * 00570000
                                      91 *      STRUCTURE FOR CREATING THE LOGICAL REQUIREMENTS AS THE "IF"    * 00580000
                                      92 *      MACRO (IT CALLS THE "IF" MACRO TO DO THE PROCESSING OF YOUR    * 00590000
                                      93 *      LOGIC). THE DIFFERENCE IS THAT AN EXTRA SET OF PARENTHESES     * 00600000
                                      94 *      MUST SURROUND YOUR ENTIRE LOGIC EXPRESSIONS, DUE TO THE        * 00610000
                                      95 *      SPECIAL WAY OF HANDLING POSITIONAL PARAMETERS USED BY THE "IF" * 00620000
                                      96 *      MACRO                                                          * 00630000
                                      97 *                                                                     * 00640000
                                      98 *      THE TWO SIMPLEST EXAMPLES ARE THE "DO WHILE" AND THE           * 00650000
                                      99 *      "DO UNTIL":                                                    * 00660000
                                     100 *                                                                     * 00670000
                                     101 *       DO UNTIL=((LTR,R1,R1,NZ))                                     * 00680000
                                     102 *                                                                     * 00690000
                                     103 *       DO WHILE=((LTR,R1,R1,NZ))                                     * 00700000
                                     104 *                                                                     * 00710000
                                     105 *       THE ONLY OTHER AVAILABLE PARAMETER IS THE "TEST=". "BEFORE"   * 00720000
                                     106 *       IS THE DEFAULT. "TEST=AFTER" CAUSES THE DO-GROUP TO BE        * 00730000
                                     107 *       PERFORMED ONE TIME BEFORE THE FIRST TEST IS MADE.             * 00740000
                                     108 *                                                                     * 00750000
